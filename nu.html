Sea Animal: The Shark

About the Shark

Sharks are one of the most fascinating and powerful creatures in the ocean. They have been around for over 400 million years, even before dinosaurs roamed the Earth. Sharks play a vital role in maintaining the balance of marine ecosystems by controlling the population of other fish species. Despite their fierce appearance, most sharks are not dangerous to humans and prefer to avoid contact.
